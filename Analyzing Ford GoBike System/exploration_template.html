What is the structure of your dataset?¶

They originally had approx 183,412 bike tours in the San Francisco Bay Area in 2019. The dataset included features relating to:

  • Duration of journey: start/end time, how long the journey took in seconds.
  • Stations: start/end station, name, latitude/longitude (geolocation)
  • Bikes rented: Bike ID.

What is/are the main feature(s) of interest in your dataset?¶

I am interested in finding out when and where day, weekday and month bikes are in high demand.

What features in the dataset do you think will help support your investigation into your feature(s) of interest?¶

In my research as well as customer relevant data, I suspect that the start time will be most exploited. I expect the place and date time to have the greatest impact on the demand for bikes.
