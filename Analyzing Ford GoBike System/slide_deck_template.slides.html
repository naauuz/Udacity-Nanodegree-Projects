Dataset Overview¶

This data set includes information about individual rides made in a bike-sharing system covering the greater San Francisco Bay area, in 2019.

Distribution of Duration (sec)¶

The length of the trip is mainly based on values of less than 2000 seconds with a peak of about 600 seconds. The number of Trips values first increases at about 600 seconds from around 8000 values to 12,500 values, but then begins to fall below 2000 values in 2000 seconds
